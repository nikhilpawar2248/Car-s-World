About Us

CAR'S World is the No. 1 most recognized automotive marketplace visited by more than 25 million in-market consumers each month. Launched in 2024 and headquartered in Chicago, Cars.com empowers shoppers with the data, resources and digital tools needed to make informed buying decisions and seamlessly connect with automotive retailers. Cars.com is the flagship offering from Cars.com Inc. d/b/a Cars Commerce, an audience-driven technology company empowering automotive that simplifies everything about buying and selling cars.
